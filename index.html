Privacy Policy — tgposting Last updated: 17.07 2026 This service ("tgposting") is a Telegram bot that allows users to submit videos for possible publication on the account owner's TikTok profile. What we collect - Video files submitted by users through the Telegram bot. - Telegram username or display name of the person submitting the video. - Caption text and hashtags provided by the user for the video. How we use this data - Submitted videos and captions are shown to the account owner for manual review inside Telegram. - If approved by the account owner, the video and caption are uploaded to the owner's TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API. - If rejected, the video is deleted and not published anywhere. Data retention - Approved videos are deleted from our server immediately after upload to TikTok completes. - Rejected videos are deleted immediately upon rejection. - We do not sell, share, or use submitted content for any purpose other than the publishing workflow described above. TikTok data - We access the TikTok account only to publish videos approved by the account owner, using the official TikTok Content Posting API and OAuth authorization granted by the account owner. - We do not access, store, or share any other TikTok account data beyond what is required for publishing.
